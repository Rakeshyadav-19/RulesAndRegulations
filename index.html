Cricket Tournament Rules and Regulations

  • 1. Innings Duration: Each team will have 7 overs to play, with a maximum of 30 minutes allowed per inning.
  • 2. College-Only Players: Only players who are current students of this college are eligible to participate. No outside players from other colleges or institutions are allowed.
  • 3. Powerplay: The first 2 overs of each inning will be designated as powerplay overs. A maximum of 5 bowlers can be used during this over. (2,2,1,1,1)
  • 4. Final Decision: The umpires’ decisions are final and binding. Players are expected to respect all decisions without dispute.
  • 5. Misconduct Penalty: Any team found engaging in disrespectful behaviour or disputing the umpire’s decisions may face disqualification from the tournament.
  • 6. Dispute Resolution: If any issues arise, they should be discussed with the organising committee in a calm and respectful manner.
  • 7. One Team Rule: A player is allowed to play for only one team throughout the tournament, ensuring fair competition.
  • 8. Substitution: In case of an injury, a substitute fielder is allowed, but the substitute cannot bowl or bat.
  • 9. Respect and Sportsmanship: All players must maintain respectful behaviour towards each other. Any form of abusive language or unsporting conduct will not be tolerated and may result in penalties.
  • 10. Team Spirit: Players are encouraged to play in the true spirit of the game, fostering a positive and enjoyable environment for everyone.
  • 11. Mandatory Shoes: All players must wear proper sports shoes while on the field.
